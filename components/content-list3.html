• Welcome to Eurydice Lit

    Eurydice Lit is a youth literary arts journal that provides a platform for young writers to showcase their work.

    • Submission Guidelines

      Yes! All of our contributors will receive $5 USD as monetary compensation for their work either through PayPal or Venmo.

    • Submission Process

      To submit your work, please email it as an attachment to submissions@eurydicelit.com. Include your name, age, and a brief bio in the body of the email.

    • Selection Criteria

      Submissions will be reviewed based on creativity, originality, and artistic merit. Selected pieces will be featured in our journal.

    • Contact Us

      For inquiries or more information, please contact us at info@eurydicelit.com. We look forward to hearing from you!

    • Follow Us

      Stay updated on our latest news and submission calls by following us on Instagram and Twitter @eurydicelit.
