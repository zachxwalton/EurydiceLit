• Submission Guidelines

    Please email all submissions to submiteurydice@gmail.com! A cover letter is preferred, but not required for consideration.

    • 1. We do not accept AI-generated works.
       
    • 2. Submitters must be between 11-24 years of age to be eligible for consideration.

    • 3. Works must be suitable for a young audience, and thus, we do not accept submissions containing violent or sexual themes, as well as discrimination towards any demographic. Work that may contain triggers should be noted in the initial cover letter of the submission email.

    • 4. We accept simultaneous submissions (we just ask that if another magazine gets to your work before we do, kindly let us know!).

    • 5. We also accept previously published pieces under the circumstances that the piece is (1) eligible for republication, (2) clarified that it is a reprint within the cover letter of a submission, and (3) can be credited to the original magazine.
       
    • 6. If a piece is accepted for publication and is unpublished, Eurydice Lit reserves First North American Serial Rights (FNASR) along with non-exclusive archival rights. If a piece is accepted for publication and has already been previously published, Eurydice Lit reserves second serial rights along with non-exclusive archival rights. Following publication, all rights revert back to the creator.

    • 7. Contributors will receive $5.00 USD as a minor monetary compensation via Venmo, Paypal, or Zelle (contributors may choose preferred method).

    • 8. When submitting, please title the subject line "Submission [Genre]". For example, a poetry submission would look like "Submission [Poetry]" and an art submission would look like "Submission [Art].

    • 9. Please include a short third-person biography in your cover letter along with your submission, preferably no more than 100 words.

    • 10. If you would like feedback on your submission, please kindly let us know in the email of the submission!
