FAQs

Commonly asked questions, answered here for your convenience.

What is the turnaround time for Eurydice Lit?

Two weeks. Although if you have not heard from us after three weeks, please feel free to inquire the status of your submission by emailing us.

Is there an age requirement for submitting work to Eurydice Lit?

Yes, Eurydice Lit is exclusive for any writer, regardless of experience, between ages 11-24.

How can I submit my work to Eurydice Lit?

Please send submissions to submiteurydice@gmail.com after reviewing the submission guidelines.

Is there a monetary compensation for contributors?

Yes, Eurydice Lit is happy to reward contributors with a $5 compensation for work, and hopes to increase this amount in the future! Until then, we hope to treat contributors to a fresh roast of coffee.

Will I be notified if my work is selected for publication?

Yes, all submitters will receive a notification regarding the status of their submission.
