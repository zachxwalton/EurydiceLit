What type of writing can be submitted to Eurydice Lit?

Eurydice Lit accepts various forms of creative writing including poetry, short stories, personal essays, and more.

Is there an age restriction for submitting work to Eurydice Lit?

Eurydice Lit is specifically for young writers aged 13-18 years old.

How can I submit my work to Eurydice Lit?

Submissions can be made through the online submission form on our website. Please ensure you follow the guidelines provided.

When will I hear back about the status of my submission?

Eurydice Lit aims to respond to all submissions within 4-6 weeks. Please be patient as our team carefully reviews each piece.

Can I submit my work if it has been previously published elsewhere?

Eurydice Lit only accepts original, unpublished work. Submissions that have been previously published, including on personal blogs or social media, will not be considered.
