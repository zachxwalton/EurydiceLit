How can I submit my work to Eurydice Lit?

To submit your work to Eurydice Lit, please visit our submissions page and follow the guidelines provided.

What type of work does Eurydice Lit accept?

Eurydice Lit accepts various forms of creative writing including poetry, short stories, essays, and more. Please refer to our submission guidelines for specific details.

Is there an age requirement to submit to Eurydice Lit?

Eurydice Lit is dedicated to showcasing the work of young writers, so submissions are open to individuals under the age of 25.

How often are new issues of Eurydice Lit published?

Eurydice Lit aims to publish new issues on a quarterly basis, featuring fresh and engaging content from young writers.

Can I submit work that has been previously published?

Eurydice Lit accepts both unpublished and previously published work, as long as you have the rights to submit it. Please indicate if the work has been published before when submitting.
