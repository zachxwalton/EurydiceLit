How can I submit my work to Eurydice Lit?

To submit your work to Eurydice Lit, please visit the 'Submissions' page on our website and follow the guidelines provided.

What type of writing does Eurydice Lit accept?

Eurydice Lit accepts various forms of creative writing, including poetry, short stories, personal essays, and more. We welcome diverse voices and perspectives.

Is there an age requirement to submit to Eurydice Lit?

Eurydice Lit is dedicated to showcasing the talent of young writers. Submissions are open to individuals under the age of 25.

When will I know if my submission has been accepted?

Our editorial team carefully reviews each submission. You will receive a response regarding the status of your submission within [insert timeframe here].

Can I submit my work if it has been previously published?

Eurydice Lit accepts both unpublished and previously published work. If your submission has been published before, please include details about its previous publication when submitting.
