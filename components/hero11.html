About Us

Our namesake comes from the legendary muse tucked away in the folds of Greek mythology, Eurydice. Although known more commonly as the lover of Orpheus, Eurydice served as a sun that drew out the best of his craft. From nymphs to gods, the vast world had been blessed with harmonic poetry and hymns from the tragic hero—a gift that would not have persevered without Eurydice’s inspiration.

In turn, we strive to be the fruit to the songbirds, the Eurydice to many Orpheuses. We are proud to be a paying platform supported by the talented words and craft of young artists from around the world. Through the poets, artists, writers, editors, and readers, we are a melodic intersection of youth, one that will continue to grow evermore.
