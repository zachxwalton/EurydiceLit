Opportunities for Youth

At its core, Eurydice Lit was established with one goal in mind: to elevate opportunities for youth to express themselves in a variety of forms. We sought not only to create a literary arts platform that offered pay to young artists internationally, but one that could also provide chances to learn internally from editing to web-design to public relations experience.

Issue 1 Open

A door has opened; whether it leads to the underworld, back to the surface, or somewhere in between, it is an invitation to explore the timeline. Hold hands with the past, wage war with the future—it does not matter to us. Simply show us your muses, and we shall show you ours.
